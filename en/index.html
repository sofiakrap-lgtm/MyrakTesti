Myrak Oy is a Finnish family business specialising in demanding facade and roof repairs of valuable properties. Our primary area of operation is the Helsinki metropolitan area.

Our clients include housing companies, foundations, parishes and real estate investment companies. We handle every project from start to finish — without subcontractors.
