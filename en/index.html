Myrak Oy is a Finnish family business specialising in demanding facade and roof renovations of valuable older properties. Our primary area of operation is the Helsinki metropolitan area.

Our clients include housing companies, foundations, parishes and real estate investment companies. We carry out every project from start to finish ourselves — without subcontractors.
